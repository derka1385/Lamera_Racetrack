# Legal Information Required

The legal pages are templates and must be completed by the team or legal counsel before production.

Required details:

- Legal company name
- Legal form
- Luxembourg registration number
- VAT number
- Registered address
- Publication director
- Hosting provider
- Contact email
- Privacy contact
- Data retention rules for enquiry forms
- Email provider / processor details
- Analytics provider details, if any
- Anti-spam provider details, if any
- Cookie and consent management policy, if analytics or marketing scripts are added

No fake company registration, VAT or address details have been invented.
