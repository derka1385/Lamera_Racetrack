FAQ

01Do I need racing experience?

Not always. The programme can be adapted, but the team must confirm your profile before any proposal.

02Do I need a racing licence?

Requirements depend on the circuit, format and event. Confirm licence details directly with the team.

03What equipment should I bring?

Equipment depends on the proposal and safety requirements. The team will confirm what can be supplied and what you should bring.

04How much track time will I receive?

Track time is defined in the final proposal according to circuit availability and the selected format.

05Can the programme be adapted to my level?

Yes. Driver level, confidence and objective shape the run plan and coaching approach.

06Can I test before committing to a race?

That is one of the intended paths. A private test can help confirm comfort, pace and programme fit.

07Which circuits are available?

Private test dates and circuits are arranged directly with RaceTrack Competition according to availability, format and driver profile.

08What happens in case of bad weather?

Weather, safety and operating decisions depend on circuit rules and the team's risk assessment. Confirm conditions in the proposal.
