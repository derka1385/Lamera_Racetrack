# Performance Notes

Implemented choices:

- Most pages are server-rendered App Router pages.
- Client components are limited to navigation state, language switching, filters, FAQ behaviour, Framer Motion reveal and the lead form.
- Images are local `.webp` assets and rendered through `next/image`.
- Missing media is handled by `ImageWithFallback` to avoid broken image icons.
- The hero uses a static poster until a real local video is supplied.
- Fonts use `next/font` with `display: swap`.
- Metadata, sitemap and robots are generated by Next.
- Motion is restrained and respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Form validation runs client-side and server-side.

Future production recommendations:

- Replace generated placeholders with optimized real photography and video.
- Keep hero video short, muted, compressed and poster-backed.
- Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` before deployment.
- Add analytics only with a real consent management solution.
- Re-run Lighthouse after real media is installed.
